Quantum Gates & Binary Shift Encoding

This application demonstrates two quantum-inspired encoding techniques:

1. Quantum Gate Operations: Simulates the application of Hadamard, Pauli-X, and CNOT gates to create superposition and entanglement effects.

2. Binary Shift Encoding: Uses quantum-inspired binary operations to secure messages with a unique visual representation.

While not actual quantum computing (which requires specialized hardware), Q-Chat visually demonstrates the principles that make quantum communication so powerful.
